Climate Change is Real - Act Now!

Climate change is one of the most pressing issues of our time. From rising global temperatures to extreme weather events, our planet is undergoing rapid changes. But it’s not too late to make a difference. Learn how you can help mitigate the effects of climate change and protect future generations.

In this section, we discuss how humans are accelerating climate change through deforestation, industrial emissions, and urbanization, and what immediate actions we can take to reduce our carbon footprint and prevent further damage.

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperature, weather patterns, and other atmospheric conditions on Earth. It is primarily caused by human activities, such as burning fossil fuels for energy, deforestation, and industrial practices that release harmful greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.

These activities are leading to global warming, which is causing ice caps to melt, sea levels to rise, and more frequent and severe weather events like hurricanes, floods, and droughts. The impact is devastating for biodiversity, food security, and human health.

Climate Change Statistics

1.2°C

Global average temperature has increased by 1.2°C since pre-industrial times.

40%

Carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere have increased by 40% since 1850.

20%

More than 20% of coral reefs have been destroyed due to rising ocean temperatures.

How You Can Make a Difference

Start by reducing your personal carbon footprint. This includes using public transport, recycling, supporting sustainable companies, and minimizing single-use plastics. Together, small changes make a huge difference!
